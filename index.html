Welcome to CHI on Tour 2018

Coventry Working Mens Club

The ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems is the premier international conference of Human-Computer Interaction. CHI has always been a place where researchers and practitioners gather from across the world to discuss the latest in interactive technology. For first-time, CHI is now coming to your city – if you want! This website is for CHI@Coventry.

At this year’s CHI@Coventry – pronounced ‘kai at Coventry’ – the theme will be CHI on Tour. Our focus will be to engage with people, to engage with technology, to engage with newcomers, to engage with world-class research, to engage with your community of designers, researchers, and practitioners… to engage with CHI!.

Weekend Jams: Feeling worn out from a week of interactive technology demonstrations, talks, and exhibitions? CHI 2018 will feature a weekend trip to the local parish fete, where jam making workshops will be held hourly by Ethel and Rose.

Join us in the lovely city of Coventry, England, and dis-engage with its multicultural population, culture, and cuisine.
